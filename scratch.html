Benefits of Reading Daily

Science backs what book lovers have always known. Here are some proven benefits of reading every day:

Builds Vocabulary

Regular reading exposes you to new words and sentence structures.

Reduces Stress

Just 6 minutes of reading can reduce stress levels by up to 68%.

Builds Empathy

Fiction helps you understand the perspectives of others.

Improves Focus

Reading trains your brain to concentrate for longer periods of time.
